生态系统学习概览 Ecosystem Learning Overview

什么是生态系统？

生态系统是在特定空间内，生物群落与其非生物环境通过能量流动和物质循环互相作用、互相依存而形成的动态复合体。它是一个自我调节的功能单位。

An ecosystem is a dynamic complex formed within a specific space where biological communities and their abiotic environment interact and depend on each other through energy flow and material cycling. It is a self-regulating functional unit.

生态系统基本特征 Basic Characteristics of Ecosystems

整体性 / Wholeness

The property of being an integrated whole

生态系统是一个有机整体，各组分相互关联、相互影响，整体功能大于各部分之和。

An ecosystem is an organic whole where components are interconnected and influence each other, with the whole being greater than the sum of its parts.

示例 / Example:
移除一个关键物种可能导致整个食物网崩溃 Removing a keystone species can cause the entire food web to collapse

动态性 / Dynamism

The property of constant change and development

生态系统随时间不断变化，经历发育、成熟、衰退等阶段，并具有昼夜和季节节律。

Ecosystems constantly change over time, going through developmental, mature, and declining stages, with diurnal and seasonal rhythms.

示例 / Example:
森林演替从裸地到顶级群落的过程 Forest succession from bare land to climax community

自我调节 / Self-regulation

The ability to maintain stability through feedback mechanisms

生态系统通过负反馈机制抵抗干扰、维持稳定状态（生态平衡）。

Ecosystems resist disturbances and maintain stable states (ecological balance) through negative feedback mechanisms.

示例 / Example:
捕食者与猎物数量的周期性波动 Cyclical fluctuations in predator and prey populations

生态系统结构与组成 Ecosystem Structure & Components

生态系统两大组成成分

生态系统由生物成分（生命部分）和非生物成分（非生命部分）组成，两者通过能量流动和物质循环相互作用。

Ecosystems consist of biotic components (living parts) and abiotic components (non-living parts), which interact through energy flow and material cycling.

生产者 / Producer

拼音 / Pinyin:

Saprotroph

fēn jiě zhě

示例 / Examples:

测验1：生态系统基本概念

"生态系统"一词中的"系统"二字主要强调什么？ What does the "system" in "ecosystem" primarily emphasize?

A. 生态问题的严重性 A. The severity of ecological problems
B. 各组分相互联系构成的有机整体 B. An organic whole formed by interconnected components
C. 生态保护的系统工程 C. The systematic engineering of ecological protection
D. 生态学研究的系统性 D. The systematic nature of ecological research
正确答案：B. 各组分相互联系构成的有机整体 Correct answer: B. An organic whole formed by interconnected components

解析："系统"强调生态系统不是生物与环境的简单集合，而是通过能量流动和物质循环相互联系、相互依存的有机整体。 Explanation: "System" emphasizes that an ecosystem is not just a simple collection of organisms and environment, but an organic whole interconnected and interdependent through energy flow and material cycling.

测验2：食物链与营养级

在"草 → 蝗虫 → 青蛙 → 蛇 → 鹰"这条食物链中，蛇属于第几营养级？ In the food chain "grass → grasshopper → frog → snake → eagle", which trophic level does the snake belong to?

A. 第二营养级 A. Second trophic level
B. 第三营养级 B. Third trophic level
C. 第四营养级 C. Fourth trophic level
D. 第五营养级 D. Fifth trophic level
正确答案：C. 第四营养级 Correct answer: C. Fourth trophic level

解析：草(生产者，第一营养级) → 蝗虫(初级消费者，第二营养级) → 青蛙(次级消费者，第三营养级) → 蛇(三级消费者，第四营养级) → 鹰(四级消费者，第五营养级)。 Explanation: Grass (producer, 1st trophic level) → grasshopper (primary consumer, 2nd trophic level) → frog (secondary consumer, 3rd trophic level) → snake (tertiary consumer, 4th trophic level) → eagle (quaternary consumer, 5th trophic level).
